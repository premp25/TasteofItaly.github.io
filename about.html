Our Story

Taste of Italy is located in Columbia, SC. Our restaurant has been serving food since January 2023. We are a newly opened business, and we hope that everyone that dines here leaves with a smile on their face. Our goal is to serve the finest dining to anyone who wishes to have a fun and a memorable time at our restaurant.
